# Code of Conduct

We follow the spirit of the Contributor Covenant to foster an open and welcoming environment.

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity/expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity/orientation.

## Standards
Positive examples:
- 使用友好与包容的语言
- 尊重不同观点与经验
- 建设性地接受批评

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insults, sexualized language or imagery, or any form of discrimination.

## Enforcement
Report issues to: open an Issue titled `[CODE OF CONDUCT]` or contact the maintainers via repository security contact (see SECURITY.md). Maintainers may take appropriate action including warning or removal from collaboration.
